FORMING PAIRS
{% endblock %} {% block content %}

  • In each round, you will have an opportunity to form a pair with another player.
  • In each market, pairs can only be formed between Group 1 players and Group 2 players. For example, two players cannot form a pair if they are both members of Group 1.
  • In each round, 2 pairs will be formed within a market. Each pair will consist of a Group 1 player and a Group 2 player. One of the Group 1 players will remain unpaired.
  • When you form a pair, you earn an amount of points that depends on the product of your multiplier and the multiplier of your partner.
  • If you do not form a pair, you earn zero.
