Player B chose to bid 10. Player B belongs to Group 1 and the other players in his group chose to bid 1 and 8. Thus Player B is the highest bidder in Group 1.

Player B will be paired with the highest bidder in Group 2. The player with the highest bid in Group 2 is player E (since he chose 30 and the other two players bid 5 and 20).

Player B's points are thus: (B's multiplier)×(E's multiplier)−(B's bid) = 8×6−10 = 48-10=38
